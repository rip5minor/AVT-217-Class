I am a level designer/world builder from Newport News, VA.

I graduated with an AS in Computer Science from Thomas Nelson Community College and a BFA in Computer Game Design from George Mason University.

As a level designer, I aspire to put most of my focus into world creation and environmental art as shown in the "3D modeling/animation" tab. I have worked with and am proficient with industry leading programs and engines such as the following:

  • Autodesk 3DS Max
  • Autodesk Maya
  • Blender
  • Unreal Engine 4
  • Unity
  • Adobe products (Photoshop, Illustrator, Dreamweaver, & indesign)
  • Visual Studio
